Common Questions

Everything you need
to know before applying

Is CampusinChina really free?
Yes — our full application service is completely free for students applying to self-funded programs. There is no application fee, no processing fee and no hidden charges at any stage.
What documents do I need to apply?
Most universities require your passport, academic transcripts, a personal statement and a health certificate. Some programs also need language proficiency scores (HSK or IELTS/TOEFL). We provide a personalised checklist for every application.
Can I apply to multiple universities at once?
Absolutely. We encourage it. You submit your documents once and we apply to as many universities as you select. Most students apply to 3–6 universities simultaneously.
How long does the admission process take?
Typically 2–4 weeks from application submission to admission letter, depending on the university. Scholarship decisions can take up to 8 weeks due to government review processes.
Do I need to speak Chinese?
Not at all. Hundreds of degree programs are taught entirely in English. If you wish to study in Chinese, most universities offer a one-year preparatory language course before your main degree begins.
What is the CSC Scholarship and how do I apply?
The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is a fully-funded award covering tuition, accommodation, monthly stipend and insurance. CampusinChina provides dedicated CSC advisory and helps you prepare a competitive application.
